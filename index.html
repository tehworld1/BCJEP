The Six Commitments

1

Immediate Teacher-Driven CCE

Support voluntary teacher-driven climate change adaptations starting in the 2024-25 school year.

2

Publicly Commit to Rapid Planning & Implementation of CCE

Accelerate climate and sustainability education adoption for all levels and disciplines.

3

Fulfill 2014 Commitment

Recommit to the district's 2014 climate and sustainability goals with updated urgency.

4

Restart Sustainability Committee

Empower a committee to oversee and assist climate education efforts with regular updates.

5

Environmental Science Option

Add Environmental Science as a mandatory or prominent alternative to Chemistry/Physics.

6

Fund Leadership Roles

Hire leadership staff and consultants to guide and oversee implementation.

Why This Matters

  • Due to the existential climate crisis and urgency to meet IPCC targets.
  • To address the unequal impacts on marginalized communities.
  • Education is key to combating climate change and empowering future leaders.
  • Pennsylvania lags behind in climate education standards.
  • No current BASD requirements for climate education exist.
